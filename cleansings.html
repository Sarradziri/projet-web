Face cleansing

        One of the most important step of a skincare routine
        to maintain the health and appearance of the skin.
        Here are some key reasons why face cleansing is important:

            Removes Dirt and Impurities
            Prevents Acne and Breakouts
            Promotes Skin Hydration
            Prevents Premature Aging
            Supports Skin Renewal.

Face Cleansing Steps

Apply Cleanser

Take a mall amount of your chosen facial cleanser and apply it to your damp skin. After, rinse your face with cold or warm water and make sure to remove all traces of cleanser from your skin to prevent any residue from clogging your pores

Toner

If you use a toner as part of your skincare routine, apply it to your dried skin using a cotton pad. Toners help to remove any remaining traces of dirt and oil while balancing the skin's pH levels.

Apply Serum:

Serums are concentrated formulations that contain active ingredients targeting specific skin concerns such as hydration, brightening, or anti-aging. Take a small amount of serum and gently massage it into your skin using upward motions. Allow the serum to fully absorb before moving on to the next step

Treatment

Apply any special treatments that can include spot treatments for acne, masks for exfoliation or hydration, or targeted treatments for specific concerns like dark spots or fine lines or under eye darkcircles.

Moisturize:

After applying serums and special treatments, follow up with your regular moisturizer to seal in hydration and provide additional nourishment to the skin

Applying sun screan

It is crucial for protecting your skin from the sun's harmful UV rays, preventing sunburns, premature aging, and reducing the risk of skin cancer. Make it a daily habit, rain or shine, to apply broad-spectrum sunscreen with a high SPF to keep your skin healthy and youthful
